## Overview

Active series count and data points per minute (DPM) are the two levers that determine your metrics bill.

## Key Concepts

- Active series: each unique metric name + label combination
- DPM: how often you scrape (15s = 4 DPM, 60s = 1 DPM)
- Over-sampling can cost up to 75% more with no dashboard benefit
- Most dashboards work fine with 60s scrape intervals
